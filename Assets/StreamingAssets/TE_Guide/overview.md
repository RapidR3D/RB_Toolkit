## <color=#FF8C00>Overview/How to use</color>
- T&E in conjunction with the mainframe is a good way to figure out if you are about to be called for a job.  
- The home page has several important pieces of information:  
- Pool Assignment (example: LO/BT F1) louisville/baltimore - Crestline pool.  
- Pool/Board position.  
- Call position.  
- POSSIBLE TRAINS (example: E70022  11/24  11:46)  
- Hours of service:  
- HOS HOURS (example: 80 hours)  
- Consecutive Starts (example: 2)  
- HOS LIMBO (example: 01 hours)  
- Attendance Points - View Details -- Select view details to see exactly what dates you received points  
- TOTAL POINTS (example: 10)  
- ATTENDANCE STATUS (example: Notification 1)  
- Credits (example: 5)
