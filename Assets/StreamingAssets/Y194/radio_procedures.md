# <color=#FFD700>Yard Operations Procedures</color>

## <color=#FF8C00>Step 1: Contact Trim Tower Yardmaster</color>
- **Channel:** 96
- **Purpose:** Notify trim and let them know which tracks you will be operating in
- **Alternative Channels:** If unable to reach on 96, try:
  - Channel 84
  - Channel 21

---

## <color=#FF8C00>Step 2: Crossing Between S Yard Sides (If Needed)</color>
If you need to cross from long side to short side of the S yard (or vice versa):

- **Channel:** 66, Tone 1
- **Contact:** LC (Local Control)
- **Purpose:** Get signal authorization to make the move
- **Note:** Sometimes the trim yardmaster will handle this for you

---

## <color=#FF8C00>Step 3: Getting Permission to Leave S Yard</color>
Once your train is built, you have two options:

### <color=#20B2AA>Option A: Via Middle Route</color>
1. **Channel:** 84
2. **Contact:** Trim yardmaster
3. **Request:** Permission off the S yard to go up the middle
4. **Next Step:** Talk to Hump yardmaster for clearance to continue south out the flood wall

### <color=#20B2AA>Option B: Via North End/Bypass Route</color>
1. **Channel:** 66, Tone 1
2. **Contact:** LC
3. **Request:** Permission to exit north end of S yard past the Colerain signal
4. **Next Step:** Get signal back south into the yard to utilize the Bypass to go south through the flood wall

---

## <color=#FF8C00>Step 4: Brief LB Before Starting Work</color>
After you start your move south with all necessary permissions:

- **Channel:** 14, Tone 4
- **Contact:** LB (Local Base)
- **Action:** Conduct a quick job brief
- **Include:** What you will be working on
- **Important:** Specifically mention if you will be working Port, as you will need signals in and out of that industry

---

## <color=#FF8C00>Step 5: During Industry Work</color>
- **Channel:** 14 (remain on this channel)
- **Purpose:** LB can reach you if you need to clear up for a passing train

---

## <color=#FF8C00>Step 6: Reporting Work Complete</color>
When all work is finished:

- **Channel:** 14, Tone 4
- **Contact:** LB
- **Message:** "Y194 reporting all work complete and all switches and derails handles are lined and locked 2 to 2 ready to proceed back to Queensgate"
- **Response:** LB will repeat and give you signals south

---

## <color=#FF8C00>Step 7: Getting Signals Into the Yard</color>
Immediately after Step 6:

- **Channel:** 66, Tone 1
- **Contact:** LC (Local Control)
- **Purpose:** Get signals from the flood wall into the yard

---

## <color=#FF8C00>Step 8: Coordinating Yard Entry</color>
After talking to LC:

- **Channel:** 84
- **Contact:** Hump yardmaster
- **Purpose:** Determine how they want you to come into the yard
- **Provide:** Train orientation information
  - Example: "Port cars on the head end"
  - Example: "All cars are trailing your train"

---

## <color=#FF8C00>Step 9: Returning to S Yard</color>
After yarding your train:

1. **Channel:** 84
   - **Contact:** Hump yardmaster
   - **Request:** Lineup back to the S yard

2. **Channel:** 96 (or 84/21)
   - **Contact:** Trim yardmaster
   - **Request:** Permission into the trim and S yard to park your engine

---
