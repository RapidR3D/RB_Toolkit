# <color=#FF8C00>Search Commands</color>  

Type any of the following into the search bar to access the corresponding guide:  

## <color=#FF8C00>Jobs</color>
- **Format**: <color=#4169E1>[Job ID]</color>
- **Example**: <color=#FFD700>Y197</color>, <color=#FFD700>Y295</color>

## <color=#FF8C00>Direct Access</color>
- **Format**: <color=#4169E1>[Direct Access]</color>
- **Example**: DA

## <color=#FF8C00>YES</color>
- **Format**: <color=#FFD700>[Yard Enterprise System]</color>
- **Example**: <color=#FFD700>YES</color>

## <color=#FF8C00>Mainframe</color>
- **Format**: <color=#4169E1>[Mainframe]</color>
- **Example**: MF/Main

## <color=#FF8C00>T&E Guide</color>
- **Format**: <color=#4169E1>[TE]</color>
- **Example**: TE

## <color=#FF8C00>Crew Life</color>
- **Format**: <color=#4169E1>[CrewLife]</color>
- **Example**: CrewLife/CL

## <color=#FF8C00>Payroll</color>
- **Format**: <color=#4169E1>[Payroll]</color>
- **Example**: Payroll/Pay

## <color=#FF8C00>MRT</color>
- **Format**: <color=#4169E1>[Mobile Rail Tool]</color>
- **Example**: MRT

## <color=#FF8C00>Road Trains</color>
- **Format**: <color=#4169E1>[Over The Road]</color>
- **Example**: OTR

## <color=#FF8C00>Union</color>
- **Format**: <color=#4169E1>[SMART Union]</color>
- **Example**: Union

## <color=#FF8C00>Miscellaneous</color>
- **Format**: <color=#4169E1>[Miscellaneous]</color>
- **Example**: Misc

## <color=#4169E1>Keyword Search Bar</color>
- <color=#FF6347>Above categories (DA/MRT/T&E/ETC) you can type in a keyword. The categories that contain the keyword will be available for selection and the keywords will be highlighted with a GREEN background for quicker reference.</color>
