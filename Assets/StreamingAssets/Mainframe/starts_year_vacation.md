# <color=#FFD700>Check Starts For Vacation</color>  
<color=#FF8C00>**Mainframe**</color>  
1. Open up Mainframe on your tablet
2. Put in your <color=#20B2AA>**EMPLOYEE ID:**</color>  and  <color=#20B2AA>**PIN:**</color>
3. Select option <color=#20B2AA>**(18 - VACATION ENTITLEMENT)**</color>
4. On the next menu select at the TOP LEFT you will see <color=#20B2AA>**OPT**</color>, put in an <color=#20B2AA>**I**</color>
5. Put in your <color=#20B2AA>**EMPLOYEE ID:**</color>  and  <color=#20B2AA>**PIN:**</color>
6. About a third down on the right you will see  <color=#20B2AA>**YTD DAYS RD**</color> and  <color=#20B2AA>**YD**</color> (example: YTD DAYS RD <color=#FF8C00>214</color> YD <color=#FF8C00>045</color>
7. <color=#20B2AA>**Add these two numbers together**</color> and that will be your current starts for the year

- <color=#FF8C00>**Note:**</color> You need 240 starts to earn your vacation for the following year
