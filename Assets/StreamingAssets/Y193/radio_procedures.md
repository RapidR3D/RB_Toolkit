## <color=#FF8C00>Operating Notes</color>
- If Trim Tower says "Clear, Clear" = Clear track, Grab birdie from bus stop, safety stop (Tie handbrake), put in clear of whichever D track.
- If Trim Tower says "Tie, Spot" = Tie on, spot at yard air.
- If Trim Tower says "Tie, Clear" = Tie on, put in Clear.
- PIVOT -> tracks 1 - 18.
- 13X -> tracks 19 - 25.
- Bus Stop -> tracks 26 - 36.
- 37X -> tracks 37 - 48.
- Tracks 14 and under are West Side.
- Tracks 15 through 49 are East Side.
- B1/E1 B1 south of underpass, E1 north of underpass (Roughly).
- B2/E2 B2 south of underpass, E2 north of underpass (Roughly).
- Write down move as trim tower explains it.
- **Note: About 22 TIES(give or take) = 50 ft**.
- Long way = North of Pivot switch.
- Make sure lights are on in track before coupling and ask permission from trim tower.
- Clusters of 3 tracks = 3-5(WEST), 6-9(WEST),  10-13(WEST), 16-18(EAST), 19-21(EAST), 23-25(EAST), 26-29(EAST), 30-32(EAST), 41-43(EAST).
- Clusters of 4 tracks = 33-36, 44-47
- Clusters of 2 tracks = 37-38, 39-40
- Single tracks = 14 & 15 leads, 6, 22
