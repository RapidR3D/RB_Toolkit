# <color=#FFD700>Check Job Award</color>  
<color=#FF8C00>**Job Awards are updated on Thursdays at 1600**</color>  
1. Open up CrewLife on your tablet
2. Navigate to **My Job** which will be the third option from the top
3. Select the "My Job Awards" section
4. A page will open with the current week at the top and the next week at the bottom, it is the next week you will be looking at. Note the job award number and cross reference in Mainframe, Bids Chart...enter number in the keywords search bar, your job award will be highlighted
