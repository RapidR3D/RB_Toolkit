# <color=#FFD700>ENGINE START</color>  

<color=#FF8C00>**Channel:**</color> Channel 96(INDUST YM 96)  
<color=#FF8C00>**Engine #:**</color> (enter engine number)  

## <color=#FF8C00>Operating Notes</color>  
- <b>_LOCOMOTIVE CAB_<b>
- Place the <b>Isolation Switch<b> in the <b>START<b>
- Close the battery knife switch closed
- Reset any tripped circuit breakers and place the control/fuel pump switch to the <b>ON<b> position
- If the locomotive is:
- 	--EMD: all circuit breakers in the black area must be on for proper operation
-   --GE:  a decal will indicate which circuit breakers must be on for operations and for operations as a lead or trailing locomotive
- Make certain that the fuses are properly positioned
- Make certain that the throttle or the MU shutdown button is not in the <b>STOP<b> position
- <b>_ENGINE ROOM_<b>
- Reset engine protective devices that are tripped, except do not reset a crankcase overpressure devices
- -- Protective devices to check
- -- --Low water button
- -- -- --Crankcase over pressure button
- -- -- --Overspeed/LOPD button
- Check engine cooling water, sight glass
- -- If any of the levels is at or below the <b>LOW<b> level, do not start the engine and record on locomotive work report. Refer to operating rules to add water
- -- If all of the levels are above the <b>LOW<b> level, start the ENGINE
- <b>_START THE ENGINE_<b>
-  Prime the fuel system
- <b>NOTE:<b> The following conditions indicate the fuel system is primed:
- -- Sight glass is full on fuel
- -- Pressure gauge (if equipped) indicates at least 30 PSI, or-
- System has been primed continuously for 30 seconds
- Crank the diesel engine until it starts, but not longer than 30 seconds 
- --<b>NOTE:<b> Hold the layshaft lever (if equipped) at 1/3 travel
- -- There may be a delay of up to 15 seconds before a GE diesel engine begins to crankcase
- -- If the diesel fails to start, repeat this procedure. If it does not start after a second attempt, contact the Mechanical Department
## <color=#FF8C00>IF AESS equipped</color>  
- Set Isolation switch to position Start/Stop/Isolate
- Close the Battery Knife Switch
- Close the Auxiliary Generator
- Close all the circuit breakers in the black panel areas of the circuit breaker panel, Close Computer Control circuit last
- Wait for computer screens on Operator Control Stand to completely power up
- Close Control, Engine Run, and Fuel Pump Switch
- Press Engine Start button
- If start fails, press Engine Start button after 60 seconds
