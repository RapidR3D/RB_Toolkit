## <color=#FF8C00>Select All Feature</color>  

### <color=#20B2AA>Overview</color>  
Quickly select or deselect all cars in a list with one tap.  

### <color=#20B2AA>How to Use</color>  
1. Locate the checkbox **above** the column of car selection boxes  
2. Tap once to **Select All** cars  
3. Tap again to **Deselect All** cars

## <color=#FF8C00>Car Range Selection</color>  

### <color=#20B2AA>Overview</color>  
Select a continuous range of cars by choosing the head and rear car.  

### <color=#20B2AA>How to Use</color>  
1. <color=#FF8C00>**Activate:**</color> Slide the "Select Range" bar to green/on  
2. <color=#FF8C00>**Select Head Car:**</color> Tap the first car in your cut  
3. <color=#FF8C00>**Select Rear Car:**</color> Tap the last car in your cut  
4. All cars between are automatically selected  
5. <color=#FF8C00>**Deactivate:**</color> Slide bar back to off when done  

### <color=#20B2AA>Notes</color>  
- Some screens have this feature turned ON by default
- Other screens require manual activation
- Available in SPT, YST, and Mobile Work Order

### <color=#20B2AA>Overview</color>

### <color=#20B2AA>How to Submit</color>
