## Select All Feature

### Overview
Quickly select or deselect all cars in a list with one tap.

### How to Use
1. Locate the checkbox **above** the column of car selection boxes
2. Tap once to **Select All** cars
3. Tap again to **Deselect All** cars

## Car Range Selection

### Overview
Select a continuous range of cars by choosing the head and rear car.

### How to Use
1. **Activate:** Slide the "Select Range" bar to green/on
2. **Select Head Car:** Tap the first car in your cut
3. **Select Rear Car:** Tap the last car in your cut
4. All cars between are automatically selected
5. **Deactivate:** Slide bar back to off when done

### Notes
- Some screens have this feature turned ON by default
- Other screens require manual activation
- Available in SPT, YST, and Mobile Work Order

### Overview
Report customer-related observations directly from MRT.

### How to Submit

2. Select the customer or junction
3. Tap **Create Insight**
4. Choose **Insight Category** and **Description**
