## CSX Customer Switch Data (CSD): The Process

### Five-Step Workflow

#### Step 1: Receive SPT
**Who:** Yardmaster or Train Crew
**Action:** Obtain Switch Planning Tool report with planned work

#### Step 2: Apply Planned Work
**Who:** Train Crew
**Action:** Follow the SPT to perform customer and interchange work

#### Step 3: Report Work Accurately & Timely
**Who:** Train Crew
**Action:** Input all work in MRT with correct times and locations

#### Step 4: Issue Work Order
**Who:** System
**Result:** Work order updated in real-time for CSD measurement

#### Step 5: Complete the Work Order
**Who:** Train Crew
**Action:** Logout of MRT when all work is reported
