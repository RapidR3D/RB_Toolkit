## <color=#FF8C00>CSX Customer Switch Data (CSD): The Process</color>  

### <color=#20B2AA>Five-Step Workflow</color>  

#### Step 1: Receive SPT
<color=#FF8C00>**Who:**</color> Yardmaster or Train Crew  
<color=#FF8C00>**Action:**</color> Obtain Switch Planning Tool report with planned work  

#### Step 2: Apply Planned Work
<color=#FF8C00>**Who:**</color> Train Crew  
<color=#FF8C00>**Action:**</color> Follow the SPT to perform customer and interchange work  

#### Step 3: Report Work Accurately & Timely
<color=#FF8C00>**Who:**</color> Train Crew  
<color=#FF8C00>**Action:**</color> Input all work in MRT with correct times and locations  

#### Step 4: Issue Work Order
<color=#FF8C00>**Who:**</color> System  
<color=#FF8C00>**Result:**</color> Work order updated in real-time for CSD measurement  

#### Step 5: Complete the Work Order
<color=#FF8C00>**Who:**</color> Train Crew  
<color=#FF8C00>**Action:**</color> Logout of MRT when all work is reported
